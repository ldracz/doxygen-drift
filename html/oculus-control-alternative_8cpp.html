void orientationCallback ( const geometry_msgs::Quaternion::ConstPtr  msg)

Callback Function for Oculus Orientation Information.

The orientation information is translated into a desired trajectory that is published to the drone.

Tilting in the y-axis when past a threshold will turn the drone left or right. Tilting in the x-axis when past a threshold will move the drone forward based on the current orientation of the drone. Tilting in the z-axis when past a threshold will move the drone left or right.

Parameters
msgThe orientation quaternion that provides the x,y,z,w orientation information of the oculus
